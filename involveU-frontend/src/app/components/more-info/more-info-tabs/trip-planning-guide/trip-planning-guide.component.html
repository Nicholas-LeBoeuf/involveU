Trip Planning Guide

Southern New Hampshire University sponsors many opportunities that occur off-campus. Keeping this in mind, participants in these activities should know that there are guidelines to follow to help them stay safe and represent SNHU positively. Although no set of rules can guarantee the health and safety of everyone involved in an off-campus activity, the University has established these guidelines to address issues that merit special attention.

Planning Timelines

  • For day trips without needing to book transportation or event registration: 2-4 weeks
  • For day trips involving booking transportation or event registration: 4-6 weeks
  • For overnight trips: 6-8 weeks
    • All trips must be approved ahead of time before any reservations will be made.

    Event Planning Checklist

    The following step-by-step checklist will ensure that all the necessary steps have been completed prior to departing for the trip.
  • Gather all trip details and create a budget and itinerary.
    • Feel free to reach out to the Club Resource Center or your organization advisor at any point in this process.
  • Submit all information through the Office of Student Involvement Trip Submission Form here
    • Upload the proposed budget and itinerary and in the form.
  • Schedule a short meeting with the Assistant Director of Clubs and Organizations to review your trip information for approval
    • Once your trip has been approved by the Office of Student Involvement, follow the specific procedures for off-campus trips, retreats, and conferences outlined below:

    Off-Campus Trip Procedures (open to entire SNHU community)

  • Make a meeting with the Office of Student Involvement Assistant Director of Clubs and Organizations to arrange and book all tickets, transportation, and other accommodations.
    • The student trip coordinator is responsible for gathering information on tickets, transportation, etc. and ensuring that they adhere to the approved budget
  • Requests for SGA vans must be submitted at least two weeks in advance to guarantee transportation; this form should be filled out in person at the Office of Student Involvement.
  • Sell tickets on SNHUtickets!
    • Submit information here
    • Allow 5 business days for all SNHUtickets requests to be posted
    • SNHUTickets requests must be made at least three weeks prior to the event.
    • Keep the ticket sale dates in mind and have all ticket sales close at least 48 hours prior to the event in order to get an accurate participant list.
  • Advertise on campus at least two weeks prior to the event. Be sure to include the following information:
    • Direct participants to SNHUtickets to purchase tickets.
    • List costs and amenities included in the trip/li>
    • List Sponsoring organization name(s) or logos
    • Include CAC statement:
      • “CAMPUS ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT – Individuals with accessibility needs requiring accommodations may contact the Campus Accessibility Center at 603.644.3118 or cac@snhu.edu”
    • Include “Paid for by your Student Activities Fee”
  • All posters must be stamped and approved by the Office of Student Involvement prior to hanging them in the designated spaces around campus
    • Refer to the Advertising Policy, which can be found in the Office of Student Involvement Handbook (pg. 7-8), for additional information about advertising (and some ideas!)
  • If the trip requires any cash on hand or payment at the trip location day of (admission tickets, snacks, etc.), submit a cash advance request at least 2 weeks prior to the event.
    • It is recommended to purchase anything you can in advance with the Assistant Director of Clubs and Organizations during your planning meeting
  • Host a pre-trip meeting with the participants prior to the trip (required for all overnight trips, but recommended for all trips)
    • Discuss trip details, itinerary, and money.
    • Advisor/chaperone should be present for the meeting
  • Fill out and submit the “Off-Campus Trip Summary” (via Qualtrics) at least 72 hours prior to the trip.

    Retreats

  • Arrange food delivery/purchase in advance with the Office of Student Involvement
  • Fill out and submit the “Off-Campus Trip Summary” if the event is off campus
    • Once approved, work with the Assistant Director of Clubs and Organizations to book any necessary events or spaces.
  • All participants (and chaperones) must sign the “Statement of Responsibility and Release from Liability” (via Qualtrics) at least 72 hours prior to the trip.
    • These forms will be sent to all participants and chaperones once the trip is booked

    Upon Return from ANY Trip

  • Each participant must return any cash advance envelope, with ALL ITEMIZED RECEIPTS and any remaining funds, to the Office of Student Involvement within 5 days of returning from the Trip
  • All purchases require supporting itemized receipts. Ask vendors prior to purchases if an itemized receipt can be provided, if not, SGA funds may NOT be used for this purchase
  • If SGA funds are spent, and a receipt is missing, the participant will be held responsible for any un-reconciled expenses
  • FAILURE TO RETURN THIS ENVELOPE WITH ALL RECEIPTS AND REMAINING MONEY MAY CAUSE THE CLUB’s BUDGET TO BE FROZEN.
